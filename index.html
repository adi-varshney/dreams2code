Our Mission

Dreams2Code is a non-profit organization with the vision of transforming our passion for computer science into education for underserved communities. Together, we aspire to spread this passion for technology through hosting hackathons, free computing classes, and aiding local businesses with digital needs. Dreams2Code strives to empower communities, fostering a more inclusive and technologically vibrant future.

Why Computer Science?

Build Projects

Our program teaches students computer science and coding, enabling them to apply concepts in projects that showcase technical skills and project management abilities.

Invaluable Skills

  • Computer science skills are indispensable today, offering job opportunities and serving as career paths and hobbies.

Fostering Creativity

  • Coding provides limitless creative possibilities, empowering students to unleash creativity and produce programs they take pride in.

Our Services

Computing classes

We teach children ages 10-14 computer science at local libraries. Students start from the basics and slowly learn how to create amazing projects using advanced programming concepts. At it's core, this consists of a 10-week training course, but can be expanded if time permits. Sign up today!

Helping Businesses

We help local businesses in the Central Jersey area with their digital needs. We create websites, mobile apps, and help with digital marketing. This is performed through our developer department and led by our Director of Technology, Milav Shah.

Hackathons and Events

We plan to host local hackathons and other events to bring the community together. This would include partnerships with local schools, and inviting possible sponsors. Please stay tuned for details.
