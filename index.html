What Do We Do?

At Dreams2Code, we empower underprivileged and disabled children by teaching them coding and STEM skills.

We also connect our students with companies and local businesses, helping them redesign websites and digital tools.

"At Dreams2Code, we equip the overlooked to rewrite their futures and rebuild their communities—one line of code at a time."

Akshat Mehta, CEO
